Our Story

La Crema is a coffee and pastry shop located in the heart of Malta, bringing a touch of Mediterranean style and flavor to every cup and bite.

Our heritage

Our story begins with a love for the rich heritage and culinary traditions of the region, which inspires our menu and atmosphere.

Revisiting Traditional Pastries

At La Crema, we pride ourselves on our unique take on traditional pastries, carefully crafted to be both tempting and delicious. From flaky croissants to creamy tarts and hand-crafted cakes, our selection is sure to satisfy any sweet tooth.

Hello I am Salvatore

Meet Salvatore, the owner at La Crema and mastermind behind our shop. With a passion for coffees , sweet and Italian tradition and a unique personality to match, Salvatore brings a touch of whimsy and creativity to every item. His journey to Coffee making began as a young boy, learning the basis and techniques from his previous jobs. As he grew older and honed his skills, Salvatore began to put his own twist on coffees.

Today, Salvatore's savoir-faire can be found in every sip and bite at La Crema. From the flaky layers of his croissants to the creamy coffees everything is a true masterpiece, reflecting his passion and creativity.

Expanding in Malta

As our reputation for quality and excellence has grown, so has our presence on the island. We are proud to have expanded to multiple locations across Malta, allowing even more customers to experience the magic of La Crema.

Whether you're a local looking for a cozy spot to relax and unwind or a traveler seeking a taste of the Mediterranean, we welcome you to join us at La Crema and discover all that we have to offer.
